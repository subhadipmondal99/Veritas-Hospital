Behind the Success

Veritas Hospital was established with a singular, unwavering vision: to bring international standards of healthcare to Kolkata and the greater West Bengal region. We believe that top-tier medical care is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Over the past decade, we have grown from a modest clinic into a 500+ bed super-speciality medical hub. Our facility boasts fully automated diagnostic laboratories, 24/7 advanced trauma and emergency centers, and state-of-the-art robotic surgery operation theaters.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, cost-effective, and compassionate healthcare services to all sections of society. We continuously strive to integrate advanced technology with empathetic human care to ensure the fastest possible recovery for our patients.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected healthcare provider in Eastern India, driven by excellence, innovation, and a relentless commitment to elevating the standard of global medical practices within our local communities.
